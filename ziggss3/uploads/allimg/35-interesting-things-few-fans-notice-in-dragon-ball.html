35 Interesting Things Few Fans Notice in Dragon Ball

278024 People Viewed - about 9 months ago Acg

No doubt, Dragon Ball is one of the most recognized in the history of anime.

As almost every anime fan has spent their time enjoying the classic series, there are still some intereting things that few of us noticed in the anime.

So, don't miss again this time.

1. Goku has perfomed the “Kamehameha” a total of 97 times throughout all three series.

2. Bulma’s name roughly translates to “panties.”

3. Bulma goes through 17 different hairstyles throughout the series.

4. Akira Toriyama, creator of Dragon Ball, named Piccolo as his favorite character, “It really is cliché when bad guys turn into good guys, but it just feels great drawing it!”

5. Goku’s mother is a Saiyan known as Gine.

6. Launch, who appeared throughout the first Dragon Ball series, never appears in Dragon Ball Z because Akira Toriyama “forgot” she existed.

7. Namekians grow at a faster rate than humans or Saiyans. For this reason, Piccolo is in fact only four years older than Gohan.
