Top 10 Sexiest Female Characters From Anime Winter 2015

165704 People Viewed - about 10 months ago Acg

Something anime has never been lacking are those absolutely stunning and sexy female characters. Hestia from Is It Wrong to Try to Pick Up Girls in a Dungeon? is the best example. 

So what we’re going to take a look at today are the Top 10 sexiest female characters to come out this season, there won’t be much description yet but you can get the general idea.

10.Masami Utoku (Rolling☆Girls)

An illustrious fighter for hire known only as Maccha Green and leader of the Hiyoshicho Propellers as well as a state Vigilante Squad Captain, Masami has a lot to offer.

She can get down and dirty in a fight or act like a cool and composed leader at any time making her one of the sexiest women in the show thus far IMO.

9.Nonaginta (Death Parade)

Not much is yet known in the series about Nonaginta, just that she has the power to make the almighty Decim bend to her will.

This is exactly what makes her sexy; her allure and mystery just add into the combination with her looks making her one of my own personal favorites this season.

8.Akagi (Kantai Collection: KanColle)

With the spirit of an aircraft carrier and the skills known to be deadly to the enemy, what makes Akagi sexy is more than just her looks.

There’s just something about a woman that can do more than hold her own!
