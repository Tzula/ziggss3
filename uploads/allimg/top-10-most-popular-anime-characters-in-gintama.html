Top 10 Most Popular Anime Characters in Gintama

1614 People Viewed - about 3 months ago Acg

The characters from the popular anime and manga series Gintama were created by Hideaki Sorachi.

When creating the series, Sorachi developed various characters based on the historical Shinsengumi, which he is a fan of. 

Additionally, other characters from the series are also based on real people. 

Here are top 10 fan favourite characters in Gintama, who's your cup of tea?

10. Kamui

Kamui is the biological son of Umibouzu and elder brother of Kagura. Due to his excellent fighting skills, he became the Captain of 7th Division of Harusame.

Hemis known as the lightning spear in the devision, the most powerful combat unit within the group. He is a major antogonist and later allies with Takasugi.

Don't let his smile decieve you, he is a ruthless beast when it comes to combat.

9. Kondo Isao

Kondo is the former commander of the shinsengumi.

When he is not busy, he is stalking Otae, earning him the nickname Gorilla - Stalker.

Although not much is know about Kondo's full strength and abilities, his nakama describe him as a fierce fighter, one of the strongest in the Shinsengumi.

Kondo is kind at heart, he is willing to put his pride on the line in order to satisfy the Amanto, he is a strong warrior in mind and soul.

8. Yamazaki Sagaru

Yamazaki is a spy working for the Shinsengumi. He considers himself "very plain" which helps him in undercover missions.

When yamazaki goes on missions he equips himself with milk and anpan.

He loves to eat anpan, once he ate it for months straight.

But most importantly he loves badminton, he will play it at random times, and even use his badminton racket for sword practice.
